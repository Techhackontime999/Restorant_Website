Our Story

Founded in 1998, La Maison Doree has been a beacon of culinary excellence in the heart of the city. Our chef, Antoine Moreau, brings over 25 years of experience from Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe.

We believe in the art of slow cooking, using only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation.
